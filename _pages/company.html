Midwest Surface Supply

Midwest Surface Supply is a streamlined, customer service driven company providing the best surface and interior cladding material available.

We believe environmental sustainability is directly tied to health. Solid Surfaces are made with safe materials and are tested by international agencies for indoor air quality, restricted chemicals, and hygienic properties.

Midwest Surface Supply is proud to be the distributor of such an incredible surfacing material.
